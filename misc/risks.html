Technology Risks

Their is a lot of risks involved in the development of medical robots it is defined as an environment that involves risk, in this field the disadvantages outweigh the advantages, One major risk of medical robots is the financial risk this meant that the average price will increase from $3000 to about $6000 for a medical procedure furthermore is the installation costs which is about $1,390,000 this in turn will cause a snowball effect on the cost of medical procedures.The building of the medical robots through the intensive labour and resources used to develop theses medical robots is a big financial burden this also has another negative effect on the medical field as they are losing their jobs due to the fact that they are being replaced by robots. As the robots are better, cheaper and faster than humans. Another risk of medical robots is the fact that there is a possibility of human error and mechanical error. The idea of latency is the time it takes for the robot to carry out the surgeon's commands. It takes a few moments for the computer to translate the inputs to the robotic arm; this means that it is difficult for the surgeons to respond quickly to problems that occur during the operation. The Human error that occurs during medical procedure involving medical robots this also lead to the side effect of mechanical issues, having human error can have a lot of risks involved like the patient in cause unnecessary damage during surgery this can be also due to the build quality of the medical robot and having mechanical issues which lead to the problem of causing human error.
